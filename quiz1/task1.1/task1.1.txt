Functional Requirements for Contact Management Application

1-Add Contacts: Users should be able to add new contacts with fields for first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.

2-Update Contacts: Users should have the ability to update existing contact information, including modifying first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.

3-Delete Contacts: Provide functionality for users to delete contacts from the system.

4-Search Contacts: Implement a search feature allowing users to find contacts by first name, last name, company, job title, phone number, or email address.

5-Merge Duplicate Contacts: Detect and merge duplicate contacts based on matching first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, or email addresses.

6-View Contact Details: Users should be able to view all details of a contact, including first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.

7-Manage Phone Numbers and Email Addresses: Allow contacts to have zero or more phone numbers and email addresses, with options for labeling them (e.g., home, work, mobile).

8-Data Validation: Ensure data integrity by validating inputs and preventing the entry of inconsistent or incorrect data.

9-User Authentication: Implement user authentication to control access to contact information and ensure data security.

10-Data Storage: Store contact information securely and persistently to maintain accessibility and integrity.

11-Backup and Recovery: Provide mechanisms for data backup and recovery to prevent data loss in case of system failures.

12-Export and Import Contacts: Enable users to export contacts for backup or sharing purposes and import contacts from external sources.

13-User Interface: Design a user-friendly interface for easy navigation and interaction with the contact management features.

14-Cross-Platform Compatibility: Ensure compatibility with different operating systems and devices to support users across various platforms.

15-Notifications: Provide notifications for important events such as adding, updating, or deleting contacts to keep users informed about changes to their contact list.
